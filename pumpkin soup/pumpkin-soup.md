# Creamy Pumpkin Soup

---

## Ingredients

- 1 cup chopped onion  
- 1 garlic clove  
- 6 cups (1.5 L) vegetable broth  
- 2 lb (≈1 kg) pumpkin  
- ½ cup cream  
- 2 tbsp olive oil  
- Salt and pepper to taste  

---

## Instructions

### Step 1
Peel and chop one onion and a few cloves of garlic.

### Step 2
Cut your pumpkin in half, remove the seeds, peel off the skin, and slice the flesh into cubes.

### Step 3
Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat.

### Step 4
Add the pumpkin cubes, sprinkle with about ½ teaspoon of salt, and sauté for about 10 minutes.

### Step 5
Pour in the vegetable broth and cook for 10–15 minutes, until the pumpkin becomes soft.

### Step 6
Remove from heat, let cool slightly, and blend until smooth.

### Step 7
Stir in the cream for extra creaminess.

### Step 8
Serve warm with croutons or pumpkin seeds on top.
